My Projects - SOLIDWORKS

These are some of my personal projects i have done on SOLIDWORKS.

QUADCOPTER Drone

A Drone Chassis with built in propellers, motors and housing for electronics. This has designed with 4 brushless DC motors and an Arduino nano. The next steps will be to purchase Electornic Speed Controller, an Inertial Measurement Unit, an RC reciver and controller. I still need to finish the coding of these devices on the Arduino IDE. If it can handle the weight some accessories such as a camera and HC - SR04 Ultra Sonic Sensor.

Balsa Wood Alarm Clock

A Balsa Wood alarm clock which can be hung on a wall. I have purchased programmable LED Strips and they have been coded so that they display the time. They can also change colours and brightness dependinding on the brightness in the room. A buzzer has also been coded for the alarm. All of this is connected to an Arduino UNO. All that is left is to laser cut the Balsa wood and assemble the housing and attach the electornics.
