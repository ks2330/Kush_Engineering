About me

Growing up I enjoyed maths. From math homework at school to helping with my parents financing and budgeting, anytime I could get involved i would. My passion for maths and engineering finally collided when the 2015 Nepalese Earthquake had devasted my home country. I fell in love with the engineering of earthquake -resistant buildings which used features such as Tuned Mass Dampeners and Shock Absorbers. I decided to follow my passion by going on to study Mechanical Engineering.

Hobbies and Interests

My hobbies include programming, 3D CAD designing, Robotics and Computer Building. I also enjoy playing football, chess and gaming in my spare time.

Work Experience
